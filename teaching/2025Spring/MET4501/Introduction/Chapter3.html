Lecture 2 Tasks

Today, we’re diving deeper into fundamental concepts of stress and strain in machine design. The tasks below will help solidify your understanding of these critical topics.

  • Read
    • Sections 3.1-3.2, 3.4-3.7, and 3.8-3.12 from Shigley's Mechanical Engineering Design to review normal, shear, and torsional stresses and strains in beams.

  • Watch
    • Understanding Shear Force and Bending Moment Diagrams: Learn how to determine and plot shear force and bending moment diagrams through animations and examples.
    • Understanding Stress Transformations and Mohr's Circle: Explore stress transformation equations and Mohr's circle through engaging animations and practical examples.
    • Understanding Stresses in Beams: Understand the stresses that develop in beams due to axial, bending, and shear loads, explained through animations.
    • Understanding Torsion: See animations illustrating the stresses that develop in round bars under torsional loads.

  • Download
    • Identification of Critical Element(s) Worksheet (Adapted from Dr. Beth Hess): Follow step-by-step instructions to locate and calculate the maximum stress in any machine component.
