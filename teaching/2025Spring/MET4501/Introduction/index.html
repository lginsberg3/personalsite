Machine Design [MET 4501]

Module 1: Introduction

Module 1 Overview

This module provides a foundational overview of mechanical engineering design through the following key concepts:

  • Chapter 1: Mechanical Engineering Design Overview (Lecture 1)
    • Introduction to the design process.
    • Exploration of how engineers develop functional products from initial ideas.
    • Discussion of creativity, analysis, and optimization in design.

  • Chapter 3: Load and Stress Analysis (Lecture 2)
    • Introduction to forces, moments, and stress types.
    • Fundamentals of free-body diagrams and their role in mechanical analysis.
    • Application of load and stress concepts in machine design.

  • Materials and Material Properties (Ch. 2)
    • Overview of common engineering materials and their applications.
    • Key material properties influencing design, such as strength, ductility, and toughness.
    • Considerations for material selection based on performance and cost.

This module lays the groundwork for understanding machine components and design principles in later modules.
